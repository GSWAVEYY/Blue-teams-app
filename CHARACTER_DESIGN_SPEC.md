# NEXUS - Character Design Specification
## Personal Identity, Difficulty, and Playstyle Blueprint

---

## üé≠ DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

**"Every character should leave a lasting impression"**

Each of the 15 heroes in NEXUS is designed to:
1. **Have a memorable name** that resonates with their playstyle
2. **Feel distinct** - no two heroes play the same way
3. **Tell a story** through their abilities and personality
4. **Have appropriate difficulty** - accessible to new players OR rewarding for mastery
5. **Create emotional connection** - players choose heroes they love, not just meta picks
