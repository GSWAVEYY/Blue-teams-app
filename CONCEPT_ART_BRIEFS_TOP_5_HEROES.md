**For Visual Artists/Designers**
**Purpose:** Detailed specifications for creating character concept art
**Format:** Front view, side profile notes, color specs, animation notes

---

## üé® CONCEPT ART BRIEF STRUCTURE

Each hero includes:
1. **Front View Description** - Detailed appearance from front
2. **Proportions & Scale** - Height, build, silhouette
3. **Color Palette** - Exact hex codes + material finishes
4. **Key Details** - Armor, clothing, accessories
5. **Pose & Stance** - How character should stand/move
6. **Animation Notes** - What moves/flows during combat
7. **Visual Effects** - Aura, glow, particle effects
8. **Weapons** - Primary & secondary weapon designs
9. **Cosmetic Variants** - 2 alternative skins
10. **Face/Expression** - If visible, emotional tone
