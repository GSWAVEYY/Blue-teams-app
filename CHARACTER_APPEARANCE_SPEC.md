### **Cosmetic Categories**

1. **Skins** (Hero-wide appearance change)
   - Full model redesign
   - New color scheme
   - New particles/effects
   - Weapon redesigns

2. **Emotes** (Victory/taunt animations)
   - Victory emote (after enemy kill)
   - Taunt emote (in-game mockery)
   - Dance emote (movement animation)
   - Sit/idle emote (pause animation)

3. **Sprays** (Territory/spray marks)
   - Logo sprays on walls
   - Personality sprays (NEXUS, faction symbol, etc.)
   - Rare achievement sprays

4. **Weapon Skins**
   - Alternative weapon appearance
   - Custom particle effects
   - Audio cues on ability cast

5. **Chromas** (Color variants)
   - Same skin, different colors
   - Allows customization
   - Lower cost than full skins

6. **Bundles** (Discounted multi-packs)
   - Skin + weapon skin + emote
   - Save 20-30% compared to buying separately
