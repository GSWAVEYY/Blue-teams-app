# NEXUS CHARACTER VISUAL DESIGN GUIDE

**Visual Design Document**
**Focus:** Character appearance, aesthetics, silhouettes, cosmetics
**Goal:** Create distinctive, memorable visual identities

---

## üé® DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

### Core Aesthetic: Cyberpunk Fantasy
- Futuristic tech + fantasy magic blend
- Neon color accents on natural fabrics
- LED/holographic effects mixed with traditional armor
- Asian + Western armor influences
- Glowing runes and digital augmentations

### Design Principles Per Role:

**Warriors**: Heavy armor, broad silhouettes, imposing presence
**Rangers**: Lean builds, ranged weapon focus, mobile silhouettes
**Mages**: Flowing robes, magical auras, ethereal elements
**Guardians**: Protective gear, shields/barriers, reassuring presence
**Rogues**: Sleek, dark, mobile silhouettes, stealth aesthetic
