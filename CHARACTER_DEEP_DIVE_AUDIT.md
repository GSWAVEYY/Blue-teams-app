# NEXUS CHARACTER DEEP-DIVE AUDIT

**Analysis Date:** 2026-02-11
**Scope:** 15 Fantasy Heroes across 5 Roles
**Focus:** Design cohesion, personality, gameplay balance, team roles

---

## üîç AUDIT METHODOLOGY

Each hero evaluated on:
1. **Character Concept** - Is the idea compelling?
2. **Design Cohesion** - Do abilities match personality?
3. **Gameplay Clarity** - Can players understand their role?
4. **Balance** - Is the hero balanced with counters/synergies?
5. **Personality** - Do voice lines match character?
6. **Uniqueness** - What makes them different from others?
7. **Visual Identity** - Icon/color/aesthetic distinctive?
8. **Team Role** - How do they fit in team compositions?
